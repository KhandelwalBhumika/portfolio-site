"TV/Web Series Negatively Affects The Viewer's State Of Mind"

Binge watch a series- with snacks-till late in the night is a very common weekend plan of youth nowadays. With the rapid changes in lifestyle, people are looking for different activities to take off their mind of personal life pressures. When they substitute TV for social interactions, they are disconnecting human nature and putting in place the virtual.

Further with the advancement in technology especially mass communication, more and more people are now attracted towards ott platforms like netflix, amazon prime video, airtel Xstream, etc. It has been observed through various studies that watching tv/series causes more damage to viewers than good. On one hand the binge watching can be a stress reliever and on the other it can be a reason for increasing obesity, reduced high brain functioning, addiction, etc.

When a new series comes up or is trending people often feel obliged to watch it at earliest giving it much more time and energy than they should. And they get so indulged that there brain quickly slips into what is called as a hypnotic state. This often becomes the reason for decreased efficiency, loss of peace of mind, lacking focus and so on. Further, it leaves the viewers mourning when it comes to an end. Apart from hindering with mental balance it equally affects physical health too. Usually while watching their favorite series people often like to snack on their favorite junk and this can become reason for increased cholesterol levels, obesity and at worst anxiety or depression. Furthermore, people don’t pay much attention to their posture while binging which becomes a cause of ache in leg, neck and back muscles as well as damaged eyesight.

Watching television is especially harmful for children. In their developmental years, watching tv/series without proper adult supervision can mould their minds thus normalizing acts like betrayal, violence, hatred, aggression etc. This will be very harmful both at micro and macro level i.e. for individual as well as for the country in the long run. Further, it severely affects brain development in a child which may become part of much bigger problems like brain disorders, deteriorating their ability to concentrate and lacking social skills. In order to lead a healthy life it has become essential that people start prioritizing there mental and physical health. This can be done by first realizing the effect of watching tv/series on their life, second by having a decent social life and most importantly by indulging in activities like meditation, physical workout, dance or any other activity which is therapeutic for them.
